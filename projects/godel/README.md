# Gödel / CEC

## Summary

Gödel is the inversion component inside the wider CEC crivello. It turns a
tension into a shifted viewpoint, a residue, and a next useful question after
the system has decomposed the tension through the other lenses.

## Portfolio Role

Gödel shows how questions become operational objects inside the D-ND operating
cycle. A user or Lab brings a tension; Domandatore expands it, Gödel inverts
the presupposition, Controprove and Proiettore test the resulting shape, and
the system can crystallize what survives.

The public Gödel page is one usable surface of this cycle. The deeper role is
internal: part of the CEC/Lab crivello that helps the system decide what can
enter memory, direction, or public output.

## What It Shows

- Public/private lens separation: visitors receive practical output, internal
  cycles use richer context.
- Tension-first decision support for strategy, research, product and personal
  decision points.
- CEC integration: Domandatore, Gödel, Controprove, Proiettore and
  crystallization form one evaluation path.
- Memory of residues and follow-up tensions.
- A path from formal inversion logic into Lab workflow and public use.
