# d-nd-seed

## Summary

d-nd-seed is the portable layer for kernels, seeds, protocols, and installable
artifacts generated or used by D-ND systems.

## Portfolio Role

It should show transfer: how a stable procedure leaves one lab and becomes
reusable in another context.

## What to Inspect

- Repository: https://github.com/GrazianoGuiducci/d-nd-seed

To curate. Strong candidate for a public card after selecting the clearest
kernels and avoiding raw operational material.
