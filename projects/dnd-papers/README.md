# D-ND Papers

## Summary

D-ND Papers is the formal/research layer for the D-ND framework, centered on
Paper Zero: the map `f(x) = 1 + 1/x`, the matrix `M = [[1,1],[1,0]]`, and the
role of `det(M) = -1`.

## Problem

The operational Lab work needs a compact mathematical source. Paper Zero gives
the kernel an explicit object: a rational recursive system whose fixed point is
the golden ratio and whose dynamics expose incompleteness as a generative
condition.

## System

The inspected material presents:

- the iterative map;
- the matrix representation;
- trace and determinant;
- convergence to phi;
- `R + 1 = R` as fixed-point condition;
- boundaries about what the claim is and is not.
