# Profile

Graziano Guiducci works on AI systems, D-ND logic, cognitive interfaces, and
agentic workflows that preserve context across long-running work.

The current focus is the D-ND Lab ecosystem: autonomous research labs that run
cycles, test claims, remember failures, expose operational state, and turn
stable procedures into reusable kernels or templates.

## Areas of Interest

- Machine learning, AI awareness, cognitive kernels, and agentic tools.
- UX-AI design, cognitive interfaces, and human-AI interaction.
- Logic, semantics, ontology, and information theory.

## Review Angle

The portfolio is meant for technical readers evaluating system design,
operational discipline, AI workflow architecture, and the ability to transform
abstract research into working tools.
